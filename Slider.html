Football

Football, also known as soccer, is the world's most popular sport, played and loved by millions of people across the globe. It is a team game where two sides, each with eleven players, compete to score goals by getting the ball into the opposing team’s net without using their hands or arms. Only the goalkeeper is allowed to handle the ball within a special area called the penalty box. The match is played on a rectangular field with a goal at each end and is divided into two halves of forty-five minutes each. Football requires skill, teamwork, speed, and strategy as players pass, dribble, and shoot to outplay their opponents. Famous tournaments like the FIFA World Cup and leagues such as the English Premier League bring together top players and fans, making football not just a game but a global passion that unites people of all backgrounds.

My favourite player

Mohamed Salah is an Egyptian professional footballer who is widely admired for his incredible speed, skill, and goal-scoring ability. He plays as a forward for **Liverpool FC** in the English Premier League and captains the **Egyptian national team**. Known as the “Egyptian King,” Salah has become one of the world’s top players, breaking records and winning numerous awards, including multiple **Golden Boots** for being the league’s top scorer. Beyond his talent on the pitch, he is respected for his humility, hard work, and generosity, often helping communities in Egypt through charity work. Salah’s success has inspired millions of young fans across Africa and the world, proving that with determination and passion, anyone can achieve greatness.
